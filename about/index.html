Hi there!

I’m a data scientist with a background in climate science, geophysics, and machine learning. I received my Ph.D. in Earth and Environmental Sciences from Columbia University, and currently work as a scientist at the NASA Goddard Space Flight Center.

I apply powerful analysis techniques to large, noisy datasets, including statistical modeling, machine-learning techniques, and high-performance computing. I work on questions related to data science, climate science, and planetary science.

My Projects page details some of the recent things I’ve been working on. If you’d like to reach out you can get in touch with me through LinkedIn.
